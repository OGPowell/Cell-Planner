Welcome to Cell Planner!

Purpose
Cell Planner exists to help you plan ahead and see days where you are over capacity.
Adding a Part to Your Calendar
To use Cell Planner, simply start by hovering over a day and clicking on the "+" at the bottom. This will open the "Add Part Form." Fill this out with the Work Order number and Part number, and then with any processes you might need to perform! For example, if you need to paint a sign and it will take 20 minutes, type "Paint" and then "20." Hit enter while your cursor is in either of the Process boxes to add another process field! Once you're done, hit "Submit" and your part will appear on its day. If you added it to the wrong day, no worries! Simply click and drag the part to the right day.
Managing Your Calendar
Once you've added a few parts, you can begin to use the calendar's features. There are three numbers at the bottom of the calendar: Capacity, Hours, and a big number that says "8 hrs." at first. Capacity is your cell's capacity for that day, whether that be determined by employee hours, equipment availability, or something else. Hours is the sum of all of the work in that day. And the big green number? That shows the difference between capacity and hours. If that is red, you have more work for that day than you have time to complete it.
Managing Your Capacity
Now comes the key component: using the drag & drop functionality to manage your capacity. If you have a day that's in the red: that is, you have more work than you are able to complete, you can begin to try and drag that work into earlier days to "load-level" your work that day.
